Discover Elegance

We craft timeless

furniture for your space

At Spares Corporation, we’ve been furnishing India’s homes and businesses with durable, functional and elegant pieces since 1984. Starting with premium in-house steel furniture, we’ve grown to offer particle board furniture from trusted partners and plastic furniture from top brands like Nilkamal, Supreme and Cello.

Our Mission & Values

We’re committed to building furniture that’s not only beautiful - but also responsible. Our mission is to deliver timeless, durable designs through sustainable practices and customer-first values.

Sustainability

We use responsibly sourced wood and eco-friendly processes to reduce our environmental footprint.

Durability

Each piece is built to withstand the test of time - strong, sturdy and dependable.

Local Sourcing

We collaborate with local craftsmen and vendors to support the community and ensure quality.

Customer First

Your satisfaction is our top priority - from customization to delivery and beyond.

We offer end-to-end furniture manufacturing, repair and polishing services to bring your spaces to life.
